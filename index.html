Instructions Below:

  • Use the arrow keys and spacebar to move.
  • If stuck press enter (return) to restart the level.
  • Grab the key and use it to enter the door.
  • Enter the door to reach the next level.

Colors:

  • Red: That's you, the Player!
  • Orange: That's the door.
  • Yellow: That's the key.
  • Green: That's a special block.
  • Blue: That's a pressure switch.
  • Indigo: That's a secret wall.
  • Violet: That's all the basic blocks around you.

Hints:

  • A certain type of block can be moved and used for many things.
  • Some walls look like they're a different color. Prehaps they can be moved.
  • When a switch is triggered, it is triggered for the rest of the level.
